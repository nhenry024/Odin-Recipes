Salad

This is a recipe to make a simple salad. It's always good to have a go to salad to keep yourself healthy. The ingredients that will be needed is listed down below

Ingredients

  • Greens of your choice
  • 4 Tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 Tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 Tablespoon of sale
  • 2 Tablespoons of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Clean the greens
  2. Mix ingredients besides the greens
  3. Make sure it's all emulsified
  4. Dress right before serving
  5. Serve and enjoy

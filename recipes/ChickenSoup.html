Chicken Soup

This is a recipe to make classic chicken soup. Good soulful chicken soup will heal anyones illnesses away. The ingredients that will be needed is listed down below

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • 3 quarts cold water
  • 1 Tablespoons of avocado oil
  • 2 Tablespoons of salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of pepper
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 4 Carrots
  • 5 ribs of celery

Instructions

  1. Clean the chicken
  2. Put water into pot
  3. Put all the ingredients into pot
  4. Medium heat and let it boil/simmer for 2 hours
  5. Shred chicken
  6. Serve and enjoy
